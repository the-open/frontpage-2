Why This, Why Now

Every day, OPEN groups enable millions of citizens take action online and offline. As progressive organizers and activists, they face multi-national challenges like climate change, bad trade deals, and eroding access to democracy.Yet the most effective means for citizens to meet these challenges are almost always at the national level, changing public policy, media narratives, and their communities.

Each OPEN member organization is an independent group working within its national context. But we problem-solve and innovate together. We share research, tools, and tactics that build our collective power. And we form bonds of trust and respect that allow our disparate organizations to become stronger together.

The Four Strands

OPEN strengthens the work of network members and helps them overcome shared challenges through a four-point program: Connect, Coordinate, Enhance, Grow.

Connect

OPEN connects groups to each other so they can share innovations, technologies, analyses, and lessons learned. These connections speed innovation and magnify impact.

Coordinate

OPEN facilitates multinational campaigns on issues that cross borders, like climate change, trade, and corporate accountability.

Enhance

OPEN enhances the effectiveness of member organizations with best-in-class technology, training and strategic analysis.

Grow

OPEN helps new organizations launch, thrive, and make a difference in countries all across the globe in record time, bringing new allies and new leaders into the network.
